Technology Review

Macbook Air (2008)

The MacBook Air is a apart of the Macintosh computers by the renowned corporation Apple. It includes a full sized keyboard, a machined aluminum case and a light pleasing structure. The MacBook Air is available with a screen size of (measured diagonally) 13.3 inches.

IPhone X (2017)

The IPhone X known as the IPhone 10, is one of the most recent but also significant phone released.. According to Apple, the iPhone X represents the biggest technological development since the original IPhone which was introduced 10 years ago in 2007, subsequently setting the path for the next decade of IPhones.

Apple Watch (2015)

The Apple Watch is a smartwatch released by Apple that relies on wireless connection to a IPhone to perform the essential aspects of what a legitimate phone would do however with seemingly easier accessibility. This could include, text, call, calculator and much more.
