Bird Box (2018)

Bird Box is a horror-thriller movie starring world renowned actress Sandra Bullock. Bird Box is based on the book written by Josh Malermam with both version recieving vasts amount of success. Bird Box is about a woman (Sandra Bullock) surviving in the post-apocalyptic world earth has now become with two children named boy and girl. All three of them have to travel great lengths blind folded if they want to seek safety.

A Quiet Place (2018)

A Quiet Place is another horror-thriller movie starring and directed by Office star Jim Halpert (John Krasinski). Bird Box was released shortly after this with common conceptions that it was a "copy". Similarly, A Quiet Place revolves in a post-apocalyptic world where rather than being unable to see, you are restrained to speak or make a sound unless you want a inhuman monster with acute hearing to rip you to shreds.
